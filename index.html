About Our Church

Our Mission, Vision, and History

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture believers in their faith journey, and serve our community with the love of God. We are committed to creating a welcoming environment where everyone can experience God's presence and grow spiritually.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in Nigeria, raising disciples who impact their communities and nations for Christ. We envision a church that reflects the diversity and unity of God's kingdom.

Our Denomination

We are a Spirit-filled, Bible-believing church affiliated with the Pentecostal Fellowship of Nigeria. Our worship style combines traditional Nigerian Christian heritage with contemporary expressions of faith.

Our History

Founded in 1985, Our Church Community began as a small prayer group of 15 believers meeting in a living room. Through God's grace and faithful service, we have grown into a vibrant congregation of over 2,000 members. Our journey has been marked by divine provision, community impact, and countless testimonies of transformed lives.

Our Leadership

Our church is led by Pastor John Adeyemi and his wife, Pastor Grace Adeyemi, along with a dedicated team of elders, deacons, and ministry leaders who serve with integrity and passion.
